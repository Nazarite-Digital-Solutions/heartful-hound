About Heartful Hound

Heartful Hound was founded and is owned by Natasha Bradshaw (known to many as Tash or Nats), a qualified behaviour practitioner with a deep passion for animal behaviour and emotional wellbeing. Based in the coastal town of Warner Beach, KZN, Natasha works with companion animals and their guardians to build strong, lasting relationships grounded in trust, understanding, and kindness. With certifications including Cert.CAB, Dip.(C.A.B.T), C.C.A.B. MHERA certified, and having completed extensive studies through COAPE International, Natasha remains committed to ongoing education in this ever-evolving field. Her experience includes working within a veterinary clinic in Johannesburg North, giving her a well-rounded, practical understanding of animal care and behaviour.
At the heart of Heartful Hound is a clear mission: to help prevent animals from being surrendered due to behavioural challenges. Many issues that lead to rehoming can be addressed with the right guidance, and Natasha is dedicated to supporting both dogs and their humans before problems reach that point.
Her approach is rooted in compassion and science. Emotional wellbeing is always prioritised, ensuring that every animal feels safe, supported, and understood throughout the training process. Heartful Hound uses only positive reinforcement methods and does not support or use aversive tools such as choke chains, prong collars, or shock devices. These methods can cause harm and undermine trust—something that should never be compromised.
Outside of her professional work, Natasha shares her life with her husband, two sons, and her two beloved dogs, Jake and Boomer. She understands firsthand both the joy and the challenges that come with living and working with animals. This perspective brings empathy, realism, and authenticity to every client she works with.

Partnerships & Professional Support

Heartful Hound is proud to be sponsored by Hills Pet Nutrition as an official Hills Puppy School Affiliate. This partnership allows us to support puppies and their guardians with trusted, science-backed nutritional guidance alongside behavioural development.

We are also partnered with Seadoone Veterinary Clinic, ensuring a collaborative approach to your dog’s wellbeing. By working closely with veterinary professionals, we are able to support not only behavioural needs but also the medical health of your companion—because true wellbeing is a combination of both.
