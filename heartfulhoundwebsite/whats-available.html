Training school

For the greatest beginning in life

What’s covered in the 5-week course:
• Positive reinforcement training of basic cues.
• Positive reinforcement training of body handling.
• Nutrition and feeding.
• Preventions and solutions to common problematic behaviours.
• Mental and physical enrichment.
• Socialisation to people, objects and foreign places.
• Health and wellness topics.

Classes for various ages

  • The Little Legends class:
    8 to 16 weeks
  • Raising confident pups from the very beginning
  • The rebel class:
    4 to 7 months
  • Because growing up comes with challenges
  • The teen spirit class:
    7 to 12 months
  • Helping teenage dogs find their focus
  • The glow up class:
    12 months plus
  • Because it’s never too late for a fresh start

PUPPY SCHOOL OVERVIEW

All programs run over 5 consecutive weeks (one lesson each week for an hour). We offer lessons at Seadoone veterinary clinic. In addition we offer private at home lessons for our clients who prefer this option. All dogs must be on lead and preferably wearing a harness which fits correctly.
