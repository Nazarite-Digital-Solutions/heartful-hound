Puppy School

All our courses run over 5 weeks, with one 1-hour session per week.

To get the best results, it’s important that you practice the skills at home between each lesson. Training is a journey, and consistency makes all the difference!

Our puppy school focuses on:

• Teaching essential foundation skills
• Building confidence and good behaviour
• Guided socialisation in a fun, safe, and supportive environment
• Helpful, easy-to-understand education for owners

Please note:

These classes are designed for puppies as well as older dogs who have not had any formal training before
